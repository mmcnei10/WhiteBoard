 * White Board is a small drawing application, that clears, captures, and saves user's drawings
 */

To clear the board simply double click.
To hide icons for better drawing, press 'h'.
To display icons again, press 's'.
You may change colors by choosing a different marker.
You may save your creation by clicking on "Save Creations", this will create a folder named "White Board Creations".
